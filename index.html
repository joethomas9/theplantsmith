About us

Wendy Smith started The Plantsmith in 2007 after deciding to transform a lifelong passion for gardening and garden design into a career change.

After completing the RHS Diploma in Horticulture and Design at the renowned Writtle College in East Anglia, Wendy quickly began working with a network of experienced local landscapers and contractors.

Her deep love for well structured design, and the beauty of plants and horticulture means that Wendy and her expert team approach each project with a depth of knowledge, professionalism and enthusiasm.
