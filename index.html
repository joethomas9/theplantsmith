About us

Wendy Smith started The PlantSmith in 2007, transforming a lifelong passion into a career.


Wendy's completion of the RHS Diploma in Horticulture and Design at Writtle College in East Anglia lead to The PlantSmith's inception, we immediately began work with a network of experienced local landscapers and contractors.


Our expert team approach each project with a depth of knowledge, professionalism and enthusiasm, as to ensure we get your vision right every time.
