About us

Wendy Smith started The PlantSmith in 2007, transforming a lifelong passion into a career.


Wendy's completion of the RHS Diploma in Horticulture and Design at Writtle College in East Anglia lead to The PlantSmith's inception, we immediately began work with a network of experienced local landscapers and contractors.


Our expert team approach each project with a depth of knowledge, professionalism and enthusiasm, as to ensure we get your vision right every time.

How we make your garden transformation easy

Our services we have been perfecting for 16 years.

Where we figure out what works best for your space, answering the fundamental questions such as 'What plants will thrive in my garden? What materials should I be using for my hardscaping & landscaping?'. Where we take your vision and create a reality.

Finding the balance between function and aesthetics. We work closely with you to understand your unique needs and vision, taking into consideration factors such as sun exposure and maintenance requirements.
